Hack 'n' Tech

Talented web & app developers, hackers and designers gather at INTERACTIVE COLOGNE to kick-off their own or others’ projects, refine or complete code collaboratively or just experiment - preferably open source.

The following program items are offered:

  • Mobile Hack Track
  • Nodecopter Hack Track
  • Rails Girls Cologne
  • Music Hack Track
  • 3D Printing Hack
  • City Serious Games SpotOn Hack
  • Open Data Hack
  • Small Hardware Hack Tracks (Arduino, Anykey)

If you have ideas for the Hackathon or want to see what other Hackers are planning, go check out the Warmup Wiki on GitHub.

Startup Business

Startups, techies, investors and entrepreneurs will meet in a creative atmosphere to discuss hot topics and network with other technology industry professionals who share similar passions, and social interests. Therefore we offer different formats:

Lightning Talks

Short talks by the attendees. Only a limited number of open slots, free choice of subject. Time is limited to five minutes.

Biz Talks

Short talks and best practices by selected startups and entrepreneurs. Meet & greet with self-made entrepreneurs in an intimate atmosphere afterwards.

Business-Model Talks

Discussions and presentations of innovative and successful business ideas and models, comparison with the hack tracks of the event. Connecting of the hack tracks „Copter“, „Anykey“ and „3D-Print“ with the business models. Question: What kind business idea can be successful in NRW/Germany?

Finance Talks

Discussions and presentations about advantages and disadvantages as well as risks of various forms of financing. Where lies the future of financing? Why Exits? Bootstrapping vs. Investors vs. public funds. Alternative forms of financing like cooperations or crowd funding. How to minimize risks and what are low-risk business models?

Web de Cologne

At the Web de Cologne event on June 20, 2013 VCs and entrepreneurs from Cologne and NRW will discuss “Sustainable Entrepreneurship vs. Exit Driven Growth“. Afterwards, Web de Cologne invites all attendees to join the networking party on the roof terrace of Thyssen Stiftung.

About Web de Cologne

Web de Cologne e.V. is a Cologne-based initiative of companies of the internet economy. Since mid-2009, its members meet regularly on networking events to discuss topics of the digital and online industry, intensify existing contacts and forge new ones. Since May 2012, the initiative is an association to represent the interests of the internet economy. Web de Cologne has the goal to connect and promote the internet economy in the Cologne area.

C/O POP & C'N'B

In the same week the c/o pop festival is taking place for the tenth time. In over 20 different venues about 150 international artists will welcome more than 30,000 visitors. The c/o pop concept, which among other things makes use of unusual venues such as the Millowitsch Theatre or the Philharmonic Hall, makes this festival an exciting alternative to the many other festivals in Germany.

C'n'B – Creativity & Business Convention is a forum where decision-makers, thinkers and generators from the creative industries’ entertainment sectors meet. The two-day event invites experienced experts and startups, company founders and dedicated freelancers, creative people and business pros, university graduates and students, to discuss future formats for production, financing and exploitation, new life and business models, new trends and visions. C'n'B offers creative people a home and an action platform - instead of passive consumption, our agenda involves active participation, and creative interaction.
