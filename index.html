Web de Cologne

About Web de Cologne

Web de Cologne e.V. is a Cologne-based initiative of companies of the internet economy. Since mid-2009, its members meet regularly on networking events to discuss topics of the digital and online industry, intensify existing contacts and forge new ones. Since May 2012, the initiative is an association to represent the interests of the internet economy. Web de Cologne has the goal to connect and promote the internet economy in the Cologne area.

The topic for this Web de Cologne meetup is “Sustainable Entrepreneurship vs. Exit Driven Growth“

C/O POP & C'N'B

In the same week the c/o pop festival is taking place for the tenth time. In over 20 different venues about 150 international artists will welcome more than 30,000 visitors. The c/o pop concept, which among other things makes use of unusual venues such as the Millowitsch Theatre or the Philharmonic Hall, makes this festival an exciting alternative to the many other festivals in Germany.

C'n'B – Creativity & Business Convention is a forum where decision-makers, thinkers and generators from the creative industries’ entertainment sectors meet. The two-day event invites experienced experts and startups, company founders and dedicated freelancers, creative people and business pros, university graduates and students, to discuss future formats for production, financing and exploitation, new life and business models, new trends and visions. C'n'B offers creative people a home and an action platform - instead of passive consumption, our agenda involves active participation, and creative interaction.
