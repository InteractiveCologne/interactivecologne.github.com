About

Classic Startup Pitches are boring - PERIOD! The same slides and the same old questions, over and over again… BUT there is hope - open your heart and light some candles for the Dating Game! Do you remember the good old times, when Rudi Carell searched the perfect partner and Susi, with the voice of an angle, matched the ideal couple?! We'll bring you back to this time! We are not searching for farmers or daughters-in-law - we present you the best and only CTO's, Founders and VC's you need to meet! Three rounds full of fun, insides and great matches! Meet your CTO! Meet your Startup! Meet your VC!
