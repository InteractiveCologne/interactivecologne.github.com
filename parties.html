After an awesome hacking and conference day we have two gigs: As Fake as I Know and academies.

Gig I: As Fake As I Know

As Fake As I know are from Cologne, or better said from the famous dance school "At the Bickys". After the end of the band "Sugoy" and the disappearances of their favorite "Dönerbuden", Elboat (guitar, lead singer), Mo (drums) and Teemow (bass) didn't become melancholic, but still wanted to keep it up. In 2009 Claas (keyboards) and Tante Boris (guitar, lead singer) joined the band, so their disco-indie-polka-music-mix made people even more dance!

Read more: http://weare.asfakeasiknow.com/

Gig II: academies
