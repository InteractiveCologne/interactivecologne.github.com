Monday June 17 HGNC Financeday

What: Conference, Biz, Finance
Date: June 17th
Time: 5 PM - 9 PM
Where: University of Cologne, "Neues Seminargebäude" (building 106)
Entrance: free

About

StartUps have the opportunity to learn more about the range of VC companies, angel investor networks, funding institutions and banks at the “Finanzierungstag” at the University of Cologne on July, 17th 2013. The investors introduce themselves and "their" topic in a 5-minute short presentation. Afterwards, founders and investors have the opportunity to network and continue their conversations in smaller groups. Founders who are searching for capital also get the opportunity to present their business models.
