INTERACTIVE COLOGNE

Hack Tech Biz Creativity — 17. bis 23. Juni

INTERACTIVE COLOGNE is a one-week festival coming up in June 2013.
Our aim is to bring Cologne’s hacker, startup and creative communities together,
promote them on both a national and international level,
as well as letting the world know how awesome Cologne is!

Hack 'n' Tech

What: Hackathon, Techtalks, Lightningtalks
Date: June 22nd - 23rd
Time: 8 AM - 11 PM
Entrance: 25 EUR (early bird 15 EUR)

Talented web & app developers, hackers and designers gather at INTERACTIVE COLOGNE to kick-off their own or others’ projects, refine or complete code collaboratively or just experiment - preferably open source.

The following program items are offered:

  • Mobile Hack Track
  • Nodecopter Hack Track
  • Rails Girls Cologne
  • Music Hack Track
  • 3D Printing Hack
  • City Serious Games SpotOn Hack
  • Open Data Hack
  • Small Hardware Hack Tracks (Arduino, Anykey)
  • Techtalks & Lightningtalks

If you have ideas for the Hackathon or want to see what other Hackers are planning, go check out the Warmup Wiki on GitHub.
