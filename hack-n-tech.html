Mobile Hack Track

We bring developers and devices together. Come down from your island to the heart of europe and join us to hack your mobile device! Independent by your skill of which if you use android, ios or windows by now.

To build bridges between technologies we support you to control some #Nodecopters with your device. Or build some awesome apps with the #OpenData API or our #small hardware experts.
